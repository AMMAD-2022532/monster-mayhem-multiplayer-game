📖 How to Play

  • 🎮 Choose a player side:
    • Player 1 = Top
    • Player 2 = Right
    • Player 3 = Bottom
    • Player 4 = Left
  • 🔮 Select a monster: 🦇 Vampire, 🐺 Werewolf, or 👻 Ghost.
  • 📍 Place your monsters on your side’s edge.
  • 🎯 Move monsters to attack opponents.
  • ⚔️ Battle rules:
    • 🦇 Vampire beats 🐺 Werewolf
    • 🐺 Werewolf beats 👻 Ghost
    • 👻 Ghost beats 🦇 Vampire
    • Same type: both are removed
  • 🚫 You're eliminated if all placed monsters are gone.
  • 🏆 Last player standing wins!
